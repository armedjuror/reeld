How it works

Two phases. Unlimited reels.

The first phase is a one-time setup. Every reel after that follows the same fast loop — no redesigning, no re-configuring.

Phase 1 — Once

Build your Reel Preset

Spend ~15 minutes locking in your visual brand. Every voice-over you make after this inherits it automatically.

Pick a background

Solid color, looping video, or image. Defines the canvas every segment renders on.

Style your captions

Font size, color, highlight mode (pill, underline, text). Typewriter, word-by-word, or fade animation.

Position the visual container

Drag to define where per-segment visuals appear. Add a frame overlay that sits behind the visual.

Add BGM & transitions

Upload a background music track with volume and fade-out control. Set a transition style between segments.

Save preset — you're done with phase 1

Share it with teammates via a link. All your future reels use this preset automatically.

then, for every new recording

What makes ReelD presets powerful

🎨

One preset, infinite reels

Background, caption style, frame overlay, BGM, and layout — locked in once. Every reel you generate inherits your brand without any extra work.

🔗

Share presets with a link

Export a preset as a shareable token. Teammates import it instantly. Everyone generates on-brand reels from day one.

🎙️

Whisper transcription

Local Whisper model turns your audio into timed segments with captions. No external API costs, no data leaving your machine.

✏️

Inline caption editing

Click any caption or timestamp to edit live. Wrap words in *asterisks* to highlight them. Drag to reorder. Undo/redo everything.

🔇

Silence removal

Configurable silence stripper removes dead air before transcription so your segments are always tight and watchable.

⚡

FFmpeg rendering pipeline

Segment-by-segment compositing with frame overlays, visual layers, animations, BGM mix, and crisp H.264 output — streamed live as it renders.
