Privacy Policy

Effective: April 20, 2025

What We Collect

When you sign in with Google OAuth, we receive your Google profile name, email address, and profile picture URL. We store only what is necessary to create and manage your account.

We also collect usage data including your credit balance, credit transactions (purchases and deductions), reel export history (duration, timestamp), and preset configurations you create.

When you upload audio or video files for reel generation, these files are temporarily stored on AWS S3 to facilitate rendering. Rendered output files are stored and served to you through the same infrastructure.

How We Use Your Data

  • To authenticate your account and maintain your session securely.
  • To track and deduct credits when you export a reel (1 credit = 1 second of exported video).
  • To render your reel — uploaded audio, visuals, and preset configuration are processed server-side using FFmpeg and Whisper.
  • To process payments and link transactions to your account via Razorpay.
  • To send transactional emails (e.g., purchase receipts) if applicable.
  • To identify and resolve bugs or service issues using anonymised usage logs.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with advertisers or third-party marketers.

Data Retention

Uploaded source files (audio, images, video clips) and rendered output files are automatically deleted from our S3 storage after 30 days from the date of upload or last access. You can delete your files manually at any time from within the app.

Account data (profile, credit history, presets) is retained for as long as your account is active. If you request account deletion, all associated data is permanently erased within 14 business days.

Third-Party Services

  • Google OAuth — handles authentication. Your credentials are never stored by ReelD. Google's privacy policy applies to the OAuth flow.
  • AWS S3 (ap-south-1) — stores uploaded source files and rendered reel outputs. Files are access-controlled and not publicly discoverable.
  • Razorpay — processes credit purchases. ReelD does not store your card or payment details. Razorpay's PCI-DSS compliance applies.
  • Supabase / PostgreSQL — stores account, credit, and preset data within a private, access-controlled database.

Your Rights

You may request a copy of your stored data, correction of inaccurate data, or complete deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by emailing us. We will respond within 14 business days.

To exercise your rights or raise a privacy concern, contact us at legal@reeld.app. Please include your registered email address in the request.

The Service

ReelD is a web platform that converts voice-over audio recordings into vertical short-form video reels. The service includes Whisper-based audio transcription, segment-level visual composition, preset management, and H.264 video rendering via FFmpeg.

By accessing or using ReelD, you agree to these Terms. If you disagree with any part, you may not use the service.

Account Creation

Accounts are created exclusively through Google OAuth. You must have a valid Google account to use ReelD. You are responsible for all activity that occurs under your account.

Upon signup, your account is credited with 100 free credits — no credit card required. These credits do not expire but are non-transferable and have no monetary value.

Credit System

1 credit = 1 second of exported reel. Credits are deducted only when you successfully export a video. Drafts, edits, transcription, and preset changes do not consume credits.

  • Purchased credits are priced at ₹0.5 per credit during the early access period. Pricing may change with advance notice.
  • Credits are account-bound and cannot be transferred to another user.
  • If a render fails due to a platform error, the deducted credits are refunded automatically. If the failure is due to invalid or corrupt input files, credits are not refunded.

Return & Refund Policy

Effective: April 20, 2025

Digital Credits — General Policy

ReelD sells digital credits that are consumed when you export a reel. Because credits are a digital good that can be immediately used, they follow specific refund rules detailed below.

Consumed credits are non-refundable. Once credits have been deducted for an export, they cannot be returned regardless of whether you are satisfied with the rendered output.

Refund Eligibility for Purchased Credits

Unused purchased credits may be eligible for a refund under the following conditions:

  • The refund is requested within 7 days of the original purchase date.
  • The credits from that specific purchase have not been fully consumed at the time of the request.
  • Only the unconsumed portion of a purchase is eligible. Partially consumed purchase batches are refunded pro-rata.

Free credits (signup bonus or promotional credits) are not eligible for refund. Only paid purchases qualify.

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, email us at refunds@reeld.app with the following information:

  • Your registered email address.
  • The Razorpay Order ID or Payment ID from your purchase confirmation.
  • The reason for your refund request.

We will review your request and respond within 3 business days. Approved refunds are processed back to your original payment method within 5–7 business days, subject to your bank's processing times.

Payment Gateway

All credit purchases on ReelD are processed through Razorpay, a PCI-DSS compliant payment gateway. ReelD does not store your card number, CVV, or banking credentials at any point.

In the event of a failed payment where your bank was charged but credits were not added to your account, please contact us immediately with your payment reference. We will investigate and resolve it within 5 business days.

For payment disputes, you may also raise a complaint directly with Razorpay at support.razorpay.com. Provide your Razorpay Payment ID for faster resolution.
